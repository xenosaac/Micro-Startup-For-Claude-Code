# Product Lead

This file is owned by the Product Lead.

Use it to record:
- current product priorities
- user needs
- competitor insights
- recurring failures and what they mean
- the next small tasks the Engineer should execute

Keep it lean.
Do not turn it into heavy project management.

Recommended structure:

## Product Thesis

## Current Priorities

## Failure-Driven Insights

## Competitor / Market Notes

## Next Tasks For Engineer
